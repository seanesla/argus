FAQ
Do attendees need to know how to code?

 No. v0 is designed for builders at every level! You'll get a mix of developers and non-technical founders, that's the point!


Can attendees submit even if they didn't attend an IRL event?

 Yes. There's a virtual track too. Your event feeds into the same global competition at Vercel Community.


Do attendees need to deploy on Vercel to submit?

 Yes, a deployed URL on Vercel is required for submission.


Can one person submit multiple projects?

 No, one submission per person. No team submissions for Vercel Global Event.

8. In Case of Emergency


Emergency Evacuation:
In case of emergency evacuation, such as for an earthquake or fire, our evacuation zone is the outside courtyard of Baskin Engineering  .

Emergency Safety Coordinators:
For this event, there are multiple people on the HackDay 2025 Organizing Team acting as designated Emergency Safety Coordinators for HackDay. They can be your first point of contact in most situations, and can help connect you to the appropriate resources. Please notify the HackDay 2025 Organizing Team in any of these situations:
Suspicious behavior or activity
Suspicious packages
Missing person(s)
Injuries
